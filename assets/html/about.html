About the site

WMSU Merch

WMSU Merch is a student marketplace project for browsing campus merchandise from different colleges and departments. The site helps students preview products, check item details, preorder merch, and track purchases in one simple flow.

Browse Products Order History

How it works

Built for easy merch preorders

01

Discover merch

Students can browse shirts, lanyards, jackets, and other product releases grouped by college or category.

02

Review details

Each item includes product previews, prices, ratings, sold counts, sizes, stock status, materials, and delivery notes.

03

Preorder faster

The cart and checkout flow keeps selected items organized before choosing a payment or pickup method.

Site features

What students can use

  • Product search, sorting, and college category filters.
  • Featured merchandise slideshow on the homepage.
  • Product detail pages with zoom preview and review comments.
  • Cart, checkout, payment method, and order history pages.
  • Login-based preorder access for student accounts.
  • Merchant and admin pages for product and order management demos.
